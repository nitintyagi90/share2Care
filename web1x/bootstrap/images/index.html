About Us

Smart Sports is a talent & celebrity management agency representing top talents, celebrities in sports & entertainment industry. Smart Sports negotiates on behalf of our clients to help them achieve their optimum potential both on and off the field. We successfully unite talents, brands, and celebrities to maximize their commercial initiatives on the national & international level. We are fast, accurate and experienced. We deliver results with integrity.

Since It’s inception, Smart Sports has become one of the leading sports & celebrity management company in India. We are a service consultancy company that represents various talents & celebrities within sports and entertainment industry. Smart Sports is very selective during its client acquisition process, which allows us to provide each client with unmatched personal attention, guidance and service. Our goal is to provide the highest quality representation and management to the athletes & celebrities who want to maximize their potential on and off the field. Our business philosophy is to have all employees contribute to a general and complete organisation based on teamwork, professionalism, dedication, and respect. For a Client this means that everybody has the necessary time and energy to cater to you. We have the necessary knowledge, tools & expertise to speed up your career.
